## 🚀 Overview
Format Factory for Windows is a free multimedia tool that rolls video, audio, and image conversion into one straightforward app. It converts between everyday formats — MP4, AVI, MKV, MP3, WAV, FLAC, JPG, PNG, and many others — and supports modern codecs like AV1 for newer, more efficient media. It also compresses files to shrink them for storage or sharing, and includes light editing so you can trim, join, or rotate clips before exporting. Provided as a 64-bit app, one Format Factory download gives your PC a capable, easy-to-learn converter that covers most everyday media tasks.

## ✨ Features
- **Video Conversion** — Convert video between MP4, AVI, MKV, MOV, and a wide list of formats.
- **Audio Conversion** — Export audio to MP3, WAV, FLAC, AAC, and more with quality settings.
- **Image Conversion** — Swap images between JPG, PNG, and other common formats.
- **AV1 Codec Support** — Convert to and from modern, efficient codecs such as AV1.
- **File Compression** — Reduce media size to fit storage or sharing limits.
- **Batch Processing & Editing** — Convert many files at once and trim, merge, or rotate clips.
